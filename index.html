The Basics

Ultimate frisbee is a bit like a hybrid between soccer and football. It's played with seven people per team on a grass, turf or sand field. Players throw the disc to one another and score by catching it in the endzone.

The Divisions

There are four primary divisions in ultimate in the US: youth, college, club and professional. At most levels, there are three gender divisions: women's, mixed and men's.

The Icons

Although success is transient for many ultimate teams, there are some teams that dominate year after year.

< BackNext >

Fury was founded in 1997 in the San Francisco Bay Area. The team won a national championship for seven straight years, from 2006 to 2012. For more, see Fury's website.

Riot is based in Seattle, arguably the strongest hub of ultimate in the world. Since 2001, Riot has never placed lower than 3rd place at club nationals. For more about Riot, see their website.

Molly Brown, in Colorado, is full of big name talent. MB has drawn power players from club teams across the country, and most recently recruited Colombian all-star Manuela Cardenas. For more, see the team's Facebook page.
